Admissions & How to Apply

Simple application process — most applicants are reviewed within 7 business days.

Application steps

  1. Create an account & choose program
  2. Upload transcripts & CV
  3. Complete short assessment (if required)
  4. Interview with program mentor
  5. Receive offer & pay deposit

Required documents

  • Passport / National ID
  • Previous certificates or transcripts
  • CV / résumé
  • Two references (optional)
Scholarship FAQ

Scholarships are awarded on merit and need. You can apply during the admissions form. Decisions are typically made within 2 weeks.
